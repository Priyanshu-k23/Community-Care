About ShareCare

ShareCare is an integrated ecosystem designed to tackle two interconnected problems: waste generation and resource scarcity.

The Mission

Every day, surplus food from restaurants, unused medicines, and essential sanitary items are discarded, while numerous communities lack access to these very resources. ShareCare provides the technological bridge between those who have excess and those who are in persistent need, facilitated by verified NGOs and dedicated volunteers.

How It Works

  1. Donate: Verified donors list their surplus items—from perishables to medicines.
  2. Claim: Verified NGOs monitor the dashboard and claim available donations on a first-come, first-serve basis.
  3. Deliver: Volunteers accept the live transportation task, picking up from the donor and arriving directly at the NGO.
  4. Track: Using live tracking mapping integration, both donor and NGO can monitor the progress of the delivery.

Trust & Verification

To maintain the integrity of our platform, all participating entities (Donors, NGOs, Validators, and Volunteers) must provide government-issued IDs or registration certificates. Our Administrators review all applications to ensure that ShareCare remains a secure, trustworthy sharing network.
